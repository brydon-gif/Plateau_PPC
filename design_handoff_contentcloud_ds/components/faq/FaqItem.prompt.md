**FaqItem** — accordion row for FAQ sections. Stack several inside a bordered panel on the gray band.

```jsx
<FaqItem question="How do languages work?" defaultOpen>
  CCBot supports cross-lingual retrieval and answers in EN/FR/DE/ES/NL by default.
</FaqItem>
```

The `+` rotates to `×` when open. Keep answers to 1–3 sentences; link out to full docs.
