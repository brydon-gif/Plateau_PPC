**FeatureList** — the red-checkmark benefit list that sits under each product blurb.

```jsx
<FeatureList items={[
  'Natural language understanding and multilingual support',
  'Full citation support',
  'GDPR and EU AI Act compliance',
]} />
```

Use `columns={2}` for long lists; `marker="dot"` for the denser spec lists on product pages.
