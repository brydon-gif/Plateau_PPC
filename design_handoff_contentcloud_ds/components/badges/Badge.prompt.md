**Badge / Eyebrow / TrustBadge** — labelling primitives.

```jsx
<Eyebrow>Representative use case</Eyebrow>
<Badge tone="red">Now in pilot</Badge>
<TrustBadge icon="🇪🇺" title="EU Data Residency"
  description="All data processed within EU borders" href="/legal/privacy" />
```

- `Eyebrow` is the red uppercase overline above section titles — Jura, tracked out.
- `Badge` pills status; keep to 1–2 words. `red` for emphasis, `neutral` default, `outline` on busy surfaces.
- `TrustBadge` is the compliance-grid tile; lay several in a responsive grid. Glyphs are flag/lock/shield emoji as on the site.
